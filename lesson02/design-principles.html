Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Crumbl

Visual hierarchy refers to the arrangement or presentation of elements in a way that implies importance. On Crumbl's site, they use larger, bold fonts for headlines and key calls to action, drawing the user's attention immediately to these elements.

Hick's Law

Apple

Hick's Law states that the more choices a user has, the longer it will take them to make a decision. Apple's website limits the number of navigation options in the menu, reducing complexity for the user.

Fitt's Law

Samsung
